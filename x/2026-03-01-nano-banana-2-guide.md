# Nano Banana (2) Image Generation Guide

Google's Nano Banana (2) offers state-of-the-art photorealistic image generation, character consistency, upscaling (2k/4k), and text rendering.

## Core Prompts & Use Cases

### Image Editing (In-painting/Global)
- **Prompt:** `Change [...] on [position] with [...].`
- **Description:** Edit specific parts of an image or the entire composition.

### Infographic Generation
- **Prompt:** `Make an infographic of this article (ratio ...): [link of your article, or upload it].`
- **Example:** `Make an infographic of this article (ratio 4:5): [link]`

### High-Quality Upscaling
- **Prompt:** `Upscale this image to [a higher quality, 2k, 4k].`

### Color Modification
- **Prompt:** `Change the color of [X] to [Y].`

### Style Transformation
- **Prompt:** `Change the style into [X].`

### Custom Figurines / 3D Print Prep
- **Prompt:** `Create an image of my own custom figurine when I was at [situation].`

### Photo Restoration / Enhancement
- **Prompt:** `Take the provided blurred photo and transform it into an ultra-sharp, realistic editorial shot without changing the composition or objects.`

### Text Rendering on Objects
- **Prompt:** `Create a photo of [X] doing [Y] on a glass whiteboard in a slightly faded red marker.`

### Style Transfer (Character Consistency)
- **Prompt:** `Generate a corporate headshot of my image in the same style as the 2nd image I uploaded as an inspiration (don't copy the face of the second image).`

### Scene Modification
- **Prompt:** `Make us surrounded by hundreds of [X] of different colors, they want to hug us.`
